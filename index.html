The Work

Body Paint

UV & neon full-body art, glow-in-the-dark performances, and custom body canvas designs that transform the human form into living artwork.

SFX & Prosthetics

Monster designs, realistic wounds, horror makeup, and prosthetic application for film, theater, and extreme character work.

Drag & Glam

Editorial beauty looks, drag makeup artistry, high-fashion editorial work, and avant-garde character design.

Surface Art

Murals & Canvas

Large-scale airbrush paintings, wall murals, canvas art, and custom home decor pieces using advanced airbrush technique.

Face Paint

Festival face art, body painting events, corporate activations, kids entertainment, and creative party experiences.
