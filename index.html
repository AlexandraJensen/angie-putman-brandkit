ABOUT ANGIE

The one with the airbrush

Angie Putman is a Calgary-based airbrush and body paint artist specializing in SFX prosthetics, full-body painted illusions, and UV-reactive work. With over 15 years of experience, she works across six disciplines — bringing the same level of detail to a film set as she does to a live event.

Her primary tool is the airbrush, and her range of surfaces includes skin, prosthetics, leather, walls, and more. Each project is approached with a focus on concept development and execution — whether it's a competition-level body paint, a photorealistic illusion, or a custom SFX build.

Notable work includes painted-on clothing indistinguishable from the real thing, custom prosthetic sculptures, and UV-reactive body art designed for stage and photography. She is listed on IMDB for her work in the film and television make-up department.

WHAT I DO

Six disciplines, one artist

🎨

BODY PAINT

Full-body painting for competition, editorial, and event work. Concept-driven pieces designed for photography, gallery display, and live performance.

💀

SFX & PROSTHETICS

Special effects makeup and custom prosthetic fabrication. Applications include film, television, haunted attractions, and themed events requiring realistic character work.

👁️

ILLUSION ART

Painted-on clothing, accessories, and textures that replicate real objects on skin. Trompe-l'oeil technique applied to body art for photography and live demonstration.

✨

UV & BLACKLIGHT

Body art designed to fluoresce under UV lighting. Suited for stage performances, nightlife events, branded activations, and editorial photography.

🦋

FACE PAINT

Character and editorial face painting for events, photo shoots, cosplay, and themed productions. Ranging from children's entertainment to high-detail artistic work.

🤰

MATERNITY & BELLY ART

Custom belly painting for maternity photography sessions. Designs range from simple to elaborate, created as a keepsake captured in professional photos.
