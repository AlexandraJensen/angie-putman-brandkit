ABOUT ANGIE

The artist behind the airbrush

Angie Putman is a Calgary-based airbrush and body paint artist whose work blurs the line between human form and fine art. From hyper-realistic SFX prosthetics to full-body painted illusions, Angie transforms skin into something that stops people mid-scroll.

Her airbrush is her signature tool — and any surface is her canvas. Bodies, faces, belly bumps, vehicles, walls, leather, canvas. If it exists, she can paint it. What sets Angie apart isn't just technical skill — it's the vision. Every piece tells a story, every surface becomes a world.

Whether she's turning someone into a UV-reactive baroque sculpture, painting jeans and a plaid shirt onto bare skin so real you can't tell the difference, or sculpting demon horns from prosthetics — Angie doesn't do ordinary.

WHAT I DO

Six disciplines, one artist

BODY PAINT

Full-body fine art paintings that transform the human form into living sculptures. Competition-level work that belongs in galleries.

💀

SFX & PROSTHETICS

Hyper-realistic special effects makeup and custom prosthetics. Horror, film, haunted attractions — if it needs to look terrifyingly real, this is it.

ILLUSION ART

Trompe l'oeil body painting that tricks the eye. Painted-on clothing, accessories, and textures so convincing you won't believe it's paint.
